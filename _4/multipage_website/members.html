Band Members

Ibrahim Ahmed Kamal
Lead Guitarist
Ibrahim Ahmed Kamal (born 2 May 1969) is a Bangladeshi guitarist, songwriter and producer. He is the lead guitarist and the founder of the heavy metal band Warfaze. He has been the band's guitarist and composer for three decades. In his early days in Warfaze, he used to play bass. In the late 1987, he took the role of playing guitar. He had appeared on all the studio albums of Warfaze. In 2005 he left the band for Aurthohin, where he played guitars. He appeared in two albums of the band, including অসমাপ্ত (Unfinished) in 2008 and অসমাপ্ত ২ (Unfinished II) in 2008. He also won the Channel i best band of the year award with the band.And with Warfaze in 2009. In 2007, he returned to Warfaze and recorded their seventh studio album সত্য (Truth). In 2018, he took huge break from Warfaze due to his shoulder injuries.
Sheikh Monirul Alam Tipu
Drummer, Percussionist
Sheikh Monirul Alam Tipu, (Bengali: শেখ মনিরুল আলম টিপু) (July 15, 1970) is a Bangladeshi musician, rhythm composer , drummer, percussionist, lyricist, producer and popularly known as one of the Co-Founder and Band Leader of Warfaze, a Bangladeshi Independent Musical Heavy Metal band group . In addition, he plays drums & percussion for the compositions and in-charge for the production of Warfaze. Best known as “Tipu” to young music enthusiasts, is one of today's producer of rock musicians. Currently he is also playing percussion with Band Pentagon since 1999-2009/2019 to till date. Pentagon used to play percussive & Latin type of music in Bangla, where ‘Tipu’ was also part of two studio albums. Therefore,the music itself resonances different. He was one of the members of the 80-90’s hit Band ‘Winning’ (1989- 98).He was a part of two studio albums of Winning which were very much popular to the Band Music enthusiasts, and he was a part of many more live concerts in country and abroad. Besides, working with three permanent Bands, Tipu also played with different bands as guest artist and also with many solo artists including POP Guru Azam Khan for two years. Presently he is the General Secretary of Bangladesh Musical Bands’ Association (BAMBA) since 2005. Besides, he is a Business Entrepreneur who is running Advertising Agencies named Carnival & Choreograph in Bangladesh. He was also the Partner & Production Director of Ektaar Music since 2001-2005.
